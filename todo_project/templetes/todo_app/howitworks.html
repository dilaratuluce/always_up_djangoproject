Sample Usage




You can classify your todos by creating your own categories. Always Up learns which category of todos is more important to you in long-term use and shapes your suggested daily schedule accordingly.

Todos have various features like length, date, priority, and colourful descriptions.


You can check and cross over finished todos and star the important ones.


You can view your daily and weekly graphs.

Always Up offers different daily schedules you can choose according to your preference. It also makes a suggested schedule looking at the priority and duration of your todos.

It pays attention to round the start times to multiples of ten so that you have more motivation to start.
